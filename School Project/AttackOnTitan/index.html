Attack of the Titans

Out | TV series | 25 episodes | 2013 | Shounen, Action, Drama, Thriller, War

Since ancient times, humanity has been waging its struggle with the titans. Titans are huge creatures, as tall as a multi-story building, who do not have great intelligence, but their strength is simply terrible. They eat people and enjoy it. After a long struggle, the remnants of humanity created a wall that surrounded the world of people, through which even the titans would not pass. A hundred years have passed since then. Humanity lives peacefully under the protection of the wall. But one day, the boy Eren and his half-sister Mikasa witness a terrible event: a section of the wall was destroyed by a super titan that appeared out of thin air. Titans attack the city, and two children are horrified to see their mother eaten alive by one of the monsters. The brother and sister survive, and Eren vows that he will kill all the titans and avenge all of humanity!
