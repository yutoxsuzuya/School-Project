Death Note

Released | Series | 37 episodes | 2006 | Supernatural, Thriller, Psychological, Shonen

Tired of boredom, Ryuk throws one of his Death Notebooks into the world of people. Just like that, just for fun, to see what will come of it. Meanwhile, in Japan, this same notebook is found on the school lawn by Light Yagami, the best student of the school, the son of a policeman. Intrigued by the instructions on the cover, he takes the notebook home and tries it out, writing the name of the criminal there. What if it suddenly works? Soon, the whole world notices the strange mass deaths of criminals, and the mysterious killer is christened Kira in the net. To catch Kira, Interpol attracts the legendary detective L, who single-handedly solved the most complex and complicated crimes. Nobody knows who L really is. From now on, in the confrontation between Kira and L, the one who will be the first to reveal the true personality of the enemy and strike before the opponent will win.
