Dragon Ball

Released | TV series | 153 episodes | 1986 | Action, Adventure, Comedy, Fantasy, Martial Arts, Super Power, Shounen

Bulma travels the world in search of the mystical Dragon Ball. Anyone who collects all seven pearls together will be able to summon Shenlong, the great lord of dragons, who will grant any one wish. While searching, Bulma meets the owner of one of these pearls, a strange tailed boy named Son Goku. Realizing that a strong boy beyond his years can become a good bodyguard for her, she decides to take him with her, and together they set off on a dangerous journey: Bulma - to collect all seven pearls and make a wish, and Goku - to become stronger.
