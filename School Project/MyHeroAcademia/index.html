My Hero Academia

Out | TV series | 13 episodes | 2016 | Action, School, Super Power, Shounen

Fourteen-year-old Izuku Midoriya learned early on that people are not born equal. And this understanding came when his classmates, gifted with special powers, began to tease him. Despite the fact that most people in this world are born with unusual abilities, Izuku was among those few who were completely deprived of them. However, this did not become an obstacle for the boy in his quest to become a legendary hero like Almighty. To fulfill his dream, Izuku, with the help of his idol, enters the most prestigious hero academy - Yuei. But this is obviously just the beginning of his amazing adventures.
