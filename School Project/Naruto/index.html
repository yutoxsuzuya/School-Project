Naruto

Released | Series | 220 episodes | 2002 | Action, Adventure, Fantasy

"This is my ninja way!" On Naruto Uzumaki's birthday, a village called Konoha was attacked by a legendary demon, the Nine-Tailed Fox. The Fourth Hokage saved the village at the cost of his life, sealed the demon in the newborn Naruto, unconsciously dooming him to life in the hatred of his fellow villagers. Despite the lack of talent in many areas of ninjutsu, restlessness and stubbornness, Naruto has a dream - to become the Hokage, the strongest ninja in the village. Wanting recognition, which he did not receive, he works hard and trains together with his teammates, Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno, as well as with his mentor Kakashi Hatake. He and his partners will have to go through a lot on the way to their cherished dreams: battles, love, friendship, betrayal, thirst for power...
